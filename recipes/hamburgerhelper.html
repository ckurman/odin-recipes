Hamburger Helper

Ingredients List

  • Ground Meat, either turkey or beef
  • Box of Hamburger Helper
  • Extra Elbow Pasta

Instructions

  1. Brown up your meat in a pan
  2. Add items listed on box(milk, water, cheese mix) and extra pasta
  3. Bring to a low boil and simmer for 8 minutes, stirring halfway
  4. I'd say enjoy, but it's Hamburger Helper
